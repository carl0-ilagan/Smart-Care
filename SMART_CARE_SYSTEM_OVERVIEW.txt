SYSTEM OVERVIEW
---------------
Smart Care ay isang real-time telehealth platform na may PWA support para sa 
Holy Infant Saviour Somos Hospital and Medical Center Inc. Built gamit ang 
Next.js, WebRTC, at Firebase.
